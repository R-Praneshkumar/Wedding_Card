Our Story
"In all the world, there is no heart for me like yours."

Priya and Arjun crossed paths at a mutual friend's gathering in Mumbai — a serendipitous evening neither had planned. What began as a conversation about the sea slowly blossomed into a love as timeless as the tides.

Three years later, under a sky of stars on a Goan beach, Arjun asked Priya the question that changed everything. She said yes, with the waves singing around them.
